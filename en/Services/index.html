Video Editing

Do you have recorded material that needs to be polished and given a professional touch? I offer video editing services to turn your footage into captivating stories. From short films to music videos, I'm here to transform your vision into reality.

Color Correction

Do the colors in your video not look as expected? Don't worry, I can adjust the color and brightness so that every frame looks the way you want. From subtle enhancements to dramatic changes, I ensure that your images are visually stunning.

Montage and Composition

Want to add special effects or combine multiple takes into one scene? With skills in montage and composition, I can create smooth and surprising sequences that will captivate your audience.

Graphic Animation

Need to add animated graphics or logos to your videos? With experience in animation, I can bring your ideas to life and make your projects stand out from the crowd.

Sound Mixing

Audio is as important as video. I offer sound mixing services to ensure that every word is clear and every effect has the desired impact. From noise removal to creating immersive soundscapes, I'm here to make your audio shine.

Subtitling and Dubbing

Need to reach an international audience or make your content accessible to the hearing impaired? I offer subtitling and dubbing services to ensure your message reaches everyone, regardless of language or hearing barriers.

Multiple Formats

No matter if your video is going to be shown on a big screen or a mobile device, I can deliver your project in a variety of formats to ensure perfect playback on any platform.

Quality Control

Need to ensure that your audiovisual material meets the highest standards? I review and guarantee that the materials meet technical and creative specifications, check formats, identify artifacts, review narrative consistency, correct color and audio, and ensure compliance with regulations and copyrights for an optimal product.
