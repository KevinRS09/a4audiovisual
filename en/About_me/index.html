About Me

Who Am I?:

Hello! I'm Nelson Rojas, passionate about audiovisual post-production and visual storytelling. My goal is to transform ideas into impactful and captivating images. Let me share a bit more about myself:

Professional Experience:

  • Creative Video Editor: I have worked on film, advertising, and television projects, refining footage to create memorable stories. My ability to synchronize image and sound allows me to bring the director's vision to life.
  • Visual Effects (VFX) Specialist: I love exploring new techniques and tools to add that magical touch to productions. From small tweaks to complex compositions, I always strive to push the boundaries.
  • Passionate Colorist: Color correction is my canvas. Through carefully chosen palettes, I can evoke emotions and set the right atmosphere for each project.

Academic Background:

Bachelor's Degree in Audiovisual Communication: My academic training provided me with a solid foundation in film theory and production techniques. I am always learning and updating myself to keep up with the latest trends.

Key Skills:

  • Video Editing (Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro)
  • Visual Effects (After Effects, Nuke)
  • Color Correction (DaVinci Resolve)
  • Sound Knowledge (Audition, Pro Tools)
  • Creativity and Passion for Visual Storytelling

My Values:

  • Commitment: I strive to deliver exceptional results on every project.
  • Collaboration: I believe in the power of teamwork and creative synergy.
  • Curiosity: I am always seeking inspiration and opportunities to grow.
